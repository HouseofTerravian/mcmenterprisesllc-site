Privacy Policy

How MCM Enterprises handles information submitted through this website.

Last Updated: March 10, 2026  |  Effective Immediately

This Privacy Policy applies solely to information collected through this website (mcmenterprisesllc.com) and any direct correspondence with MCM Enterprises. It does not govern the data practices of any individual operating entity within the MCM Enterprises portfolio. Each operating entity maintains its own privacy policy, which governs the data it independently collects from its users and customers.

1. Who We Are

MCM Enterprises is a management and marketing company operating as a DBA under SlateRiver LLC, a New Mexico limited liability company. We do not sell products or services directly to consumers. This website serves as an identity and informational resource for vendors, partners, financial institutions, and other business contacts.

References to "MCM Enterprises," "we," "us," or "our" in this policy refer to MCM Enterprises and SlateRiver LLC collectively.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the information you voluntarily provide when you submit an inquiry through the contact form on this website. This may include:

  • First and last name
  • Email address
  • Organization or company name (if provided)
  • Inquiry type selection
  • The content of your message

We do not collect any automatically generated tracking data, cookies, or analytics through this website at this time. We do not collect payment information, sensitive personal information, or health-related data through this website.

3. How We Use Your Information

Information submitted through the contact form is used exclusively to:

  • Respond to your inquiry and communicate with you regarding your request;
  • Route your inquiry to the appropriate internal team or operating entity;
  • Maintain records of business communications for operational and legal purposes.

We do not use your information for marketing purposes, and we do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

4. Your Relationship Is with the Operating Entity

MCM Enterprises functions solely as a management intermediary. If your inquiry is routed to a specific operating entity within our portfolio, that entity's own privacy policy will govern the handling of any information you subsequently share with them directly. MCM Enterprises is not responsible for the data practices of those individual entities.

You are encouraged to review the privacy policy of any operating entity before sharing personal, financial, or health-related information with that entity.

5. Third-Party Form Processing

The contact form on this website is processed through Formspree, a third-party form submission service. By submitting the contact form, your submission is transmitted to and stored by Formspree on our behalf. Formspree's handling of this data is governed by their own privacy policy, available at formspree.io. MCM Enterprises does not share your form submission data with any other third parties.

6. Data Retention

We retain contact form submissions and related correspondence for as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose for which they were submitted, and as required by applicable law or for legitimate business recordkeeping purposes. Inquiries that do not result in an active business relationship are generally retained for no longer than two (2) years.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect the information you submit from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure. By submitting information through this website, you acknowledge and accept this inherent risk.

8. Children's Privacy

This website is intended for business and professional use only. MCM Enterprises does not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under the age of 18. If you believe a minor has submitted information through this website, please contact us immediately so we can remove it.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have certain rights with respect to the personal information we hold about you, including the right to access, correct, or request deletion of that information. To exercise any such rights, please contact us using the contact form and select "General Business Inquiry."

Please note that any dispute regarding this Privacy Policy or the handling of your information is subject to the mandatory arbitration provisions set forth in our Terms of Use.

10. Healthcare Communications (Muonelo Communications)

Muonelo Communications, a division of MCM Enterprises focused on HIPAA-related communications, operates under separate and enhanced data handling protocols consistent with applicable healthcare privacy regulations. Inquiries submitted through this website that relate to healthcare communications matters will be handled in accordance with those protocols. No protected health information (PHI) should be submitted through this general contact form.

11. Modifications to This Policy

MCM Enterprises reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of this website following any modification constitutes your acceptance of the revised Policy.

12. Contact

For privacy-related questions or requests, please use the contact form on this website and select "General Business Inquiry" as your inquiry type.
